AI in Dentistry | Mydentist.AI


WHO in a recent statement recommended people not to go for dental appointments if it is not urgent because of the COVID-19 pandemic. Today, dentists are facing a difficult time. The nature of their work puts them at a high risk of contracting and transmitting viral diseases like COVID-19. The patients, doctors and the whole healthcare ecosystem is making strenuous efforts to keep up with the current scenario. There has been a sharp decline in the influx of patients seeking dental care in the last few months, the situation seems to be getting worse day by day.
